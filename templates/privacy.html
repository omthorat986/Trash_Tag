Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

Trashtag Bag ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Trashtag Bag website and mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

  • Personal Data: While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, and location data.
  • Usage Data: We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include your computer's IP address, browser type, and the pages of our Service that you visit.
  • Photographic Data: To verify cleanups and operate our rewards program, we require you to submit "before and after" photographs. These photographs may contain location metadata.

2. How We Use Your Data

Trashtag Bag uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • To manage your account and verify your cleanup activities.
  • To administer the rewards program.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and sending service-related announcements.
  • To showcase community impact on our platform and in marketing materials (with your consent where required).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our Service, who are obligated not to disclose or use your information for any other purpose.
  • For Legal Requirements: We may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect the security of the Service, or protect the personal safety of users.
  • Publicly: With your explicit consent, we may display your cleanup photos, first name, and general location (e.g., "Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra") on our public-facing leaderboards or promotional materials.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can do this at any time by accessing your account settings or by contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at info@trashtagbag.com.
